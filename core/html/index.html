01 Ingredients

Created for Your Healthiest Skin.

Most of our concept ingredients have been widely used in hanbang medicines.
Fine hanbang ingredients help to soothe and heal the fatigued skin.

Ginseng

A prized Hanbang ingredient, Ginseng is packed with antioxidants and saponins, helping to firm, energize, and brighten tired skin. Known for its anti-aging properties, it enhances circulation and restores a youthful glow.

  • Boost Elasticity
  • Brightens your skin
  • Anti-aging
  • Exfoliates

Rice Line

A staple of Korean skincare for centuries, Rice is rich in amino acids, vitamins, and antioxidants. It helps to gently brighten dull skin, improve texture, and deeply hydrate leaving your complexion soft, smooth, and luminous.

  • Hydrates
  • Brightens
  • Improves texture
  • Anti-flammatory properties

Green Plum

Known for its natural AHA content, Green Plum gently exfoliates to smooth skin texture and unclog pores. It helps reveal clearer, more refined skin while maintaining a soft, hydrated finish. It originates from korea.

  • Exfoliates
  • Clarifies
  • Smoothes out
  • Unclogs pores
